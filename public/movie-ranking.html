The Best Movies According to Sid

My top 3 Movies of all-time

URI - The Surgical Strike

This movie is based on India's 1st Counter Terrorism Operation in September 2019 in Pakistan Occupied Kashmir (PoK)
after Pakistani Terrorists killed 40 Indian Soldiers in a deadly & cowardly attack.

Major

This movie is based on a real life incident of Major Sandeep Unnikrishnan of NSG (National Security Guard)
who got martyred in 26/11 Mumbai Terror Attacks at Taj Hotel in 2008 while nuteralizing the terrorists all by himself.

Kedarnath

This is a movie based on real life incident of 2013 floods in Kedarnath. And an incomplete love story of a muslim
boy & a hindu girl.
